# Raw Documents Directory

Place your Wisconsin legal documents in the following subdirectories:

- `statutes/` - Wisconsin State Statutes (PDF/HTML format)
- `case_law/` - Case law summaries (PDF format)
- `policies/` - Department policies (DOCX/PDF format)
- `training/` - Training materials (various formats)

## Sample Documents

You can use publicly available Wisconsin legal documents from:
- Wisconsin State Legislature: https://docs.legis.wisconsin.gov/statutes
- Wisconsin Court System: https://www.wicourts.gov/
